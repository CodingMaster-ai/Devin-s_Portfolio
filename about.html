Who Am I?

Hello, I’m Devin Sloan, an entry-level Front-End Developer with a graphic design background. Born and raised in Orlando, FL, I enjoy origami, designing, and learning languages in my free time.


My passion for technology began in 7th grade during an ICT 2 class, where I was introduced to HTML. Inspired, I taught myself basic web development through a YouTube video, which sparked my interest in pursuing a career in tech.


In 2022, I considered enrolling in a Web Development program at Orange Technical College but chose the Digital Media/Multimedia Design program instead, which included web design. I graduated in 2024, creating designs I’m proud of, some of which are featured in my physical portfolio and on Instagram. After graduation, I explored graphic design careers but realized my true passion lies in web development.


I enrolled in Codecademy’s Front-End Engineer course, where the structured approach, quizzes, and guided projects have helped me improve rapidly. I am continuing my studies in Front-End Development and I am confident that Codecademy is helping me pave the way to becoming a successful Front-End Developer.
